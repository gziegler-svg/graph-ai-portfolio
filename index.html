My Vision

Visionary Leadership in Education

As an Assistant Professor at Harford Community College and a PhD candidate at Liberty University, my focus is on demystifying AI for healthcare professionals. My dissertation explores the impact of interactive AI avatars on critical thinking in undergraduate nursing students.


I believe AI is a catalyst for transforming how we cultivate clinical judgment. This portfolio documents my journey through the "Generative AI for Work & Research" cohort, where I am mastering no-code tools to automate tasks and enhance research productivity.
